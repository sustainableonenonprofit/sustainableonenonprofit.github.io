Our GoFundMe to Save Australian Wildlife

Goal: Raise $3000 for the International Fund for Animal Welfare (IFAW)

Since November 2019, the Australian bushfires have destroyed many natural habitats. Australia needs your help. With our mission to protect our environment, we created this GoFundMe to help our wildlife friends in Australia!

As a thank you for your support...
Donations of $10 or more will receive a laminated koala sticker
Donations of $50 or more will also receive a koala tote bag

***Important Note: Please be sure to send us a message with your address! Stickers & tote bags can only be shipped to addresses in continental United States.***

All proceeds will be directly donated to the International Fund for Animal Welfare (IFAW), a nonprofit organization. IFAW uses the donations to supply food and purchase veterinary supplies for koalas, kangaroos, and other animals rescued from the bushfires. You can read more about the organization on their website here.

Please help us reach our goal by sharing this fundraiser! Any amount donated is greatly appreciated! We can make a difference with your help.

A Campaign - First Blog Post

Welcome to Sustainable One's Blog! Our environment matters, and through our youth campaign to spread environmental awareness and fundraise to support organizations focused on saving the environment. We need your help in making our mission successful.

Our blog will update you with news and stories! Subscribe to our newsletter to see when there is a new posting and to find out how you can decrease your carbon footprint.

Some things we have planned include creating helpful infographics on environmental facts and publishing blog posts. We are working on making the website as user-friendly as possible, so stick around!

It only takes one idea to start a movement. Sustainable One hopes to encourage everyone to live a more sustainable life. We CAN do something.
