Apps to Save the Earth

Published December 2019

Wondering how you can make a difference every day?

Did you know that web browsing could help plant trees? By using the money earned from ads to plant trees, apps like Ecosia are changing the world. Check out this article that features various apps to help you save the planet from the App Store. Some of these apps can help match you to environmental volunteering activities, track how much trash you pick up, and monitor your carbon footprint!

In addition, as mentioned on the solution to food waste article we previously published, check out this article from Impakter to learn about apps that can help reduce food waste. These apps are good to learn about for individuals and local businesses.
