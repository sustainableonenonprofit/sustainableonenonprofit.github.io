Mission Statement

____________________________

As a student-led nonprofit based in Massachusetts, we hope to engage our community and beyond in protecting our environment. We seek to increase environmental education and awareness through fundraising, hosting workshops for students, producing informative video content, writing articles, and other related means. Profits raised from our fundraising will be donated to nonprofit organizations that are actively helping to save the planet.


Why Sustainability is Important

Our actions matter. Check out our Learn section to read articles and view infographics our group has made and to discover extra online resources on all things environmental.


Products

We sell products handmade by us, a group of high school students. From stylish stickers to encourage your reusable water bottle usage to reusable bags for shopping, our products will help you reduce your carbon footprint.

Funds earned goes to organizations saving the environment.


What We Do

We hope to raise awareness of environmental issues through our fundraisers. We are currently working on creating environmental education resources and advocating for a more sustainable lifestyle through social media. In the future, we hope to host a local conference for high school students. For more information, check out our About page here! To see what we have been doing lately, check out our blog!
